              <h1 class="ff-global-title">Finding free resources</h1>
              <div class="ff-post ff-post-editable ff-post-new">
                <div class="ff-post-content">
                  <div class="ff-container" id="ffContainer">
                    <div data-ff-component-type="html" class="  odd component ff-component ff-component-type-html" id="ffComponent_126092"><p>Because Scheduler holds a unified database of what's going on, it can also give you a listing of who (or what) isn't occupied in any given time interval. &nbsp;This can be useful for finding a free classroom, a free colleague, or free students. &nbsp;You can also use it in conjunction with your own group definitions to find free anythings.</p><p>To use this facility, click on the word "Menu" at top right, and choose "Find free". &nbsp;You should see a screen something like this:</p></div>
                    <div data-ff-component-type="image" class="  ff-component-block even component ff-component ff-component-type-image" id="ffComponent_126309"><figure class="ff-image"><img src="./Finding free resources - Abingdon_files/resource(1).aspx" data-ff-nativeheight="1000" data-ff-nativewidth="1258"></figure></div>
                    <div data-ff-component-type="html" class="  odd component ff-component ff-component-type-html" id="ffComponent_126315"><p>In the "group name" field you can type the name of any kind of group - a teaching group or tutor group, a tag group, an Other Half group, one of the pre-populated system groups - see&nbsp;<a href="http://abingdon.fireflycloud.net/ict-and-elearning/scheduler/using-groups/pre-populated-groups">Pre-populated groups</a>&nbsp;for a list - or a group of your own creation. &nbsp;Here I start to type "Mathematics teachers", and a list of matching groups appears.</p></div>
                    <div data-ff-component-type="image" class="  ff-component-block even component ff-component ff-component-type-image" id="ffComponent_126316"><figure class="ff-image"><img src="./Finding free resources - Abingdon_files/resource(2).aspx" data-ff-nativeheight="1000" data-ff-nativewidth="1258"></figure></div>
                    <div data-ff-component-type="html" class="  odd component ff-component ff-component-type-html" id="ffComponent_126327"><p>I moved the highlight down to "Mathematics teachers" and pressed enter to select it. &nbsp;Then I clicked on 6 in the periods bar to specify period 6 (the system understands different period times on different days) and finally clicked on "Find free". &nbsp;There are apparently two maths teachers free at that time. &nbsp;The system takes account not just of the timetable, but also any other commitments which have been entered, of whatever type.</p></div>
                    <div data-ff-component-type="image" class="  ff-component-block even component ff-component ff-component-type-image" id="ffComponent_126322"><figure class="ff-image"><img src="./Finding free resources - Abingdon_files/resource(3).aspx" data-ff-nativeheight="1000" data-ff-nativewidth="1258"></figure></div>
                    <div data-ff-component-type="html" class="  odd component ff-component ff-component-type-html" id="ffComponent_126330"><p>Having got the list, two new buttons have appeared. &nbsp;The "Export" button allows you to download the list as a CSV file, suitable for loading into a spreadsheet. &nbsp;The "Create group" button will create a new group for you, populated with the resources which you just found.</p></div>
                  </div>
